FUTURISTIC MEGA CITY — FULL RULEBOOK (UPDATED) OBJECTIVE Be the last player financially standing in the Mega City by managing money, properties, teleport cards, energy, quantum tokens, and strategic movement. TURN ORDER 1. Roll the dice. 2. Move your piece forward the number of spaces rolled. 3. Resolve the space you land on. 4. Pay or earn money depending on the space. 5. Optional: use an item, teleport card, or upgrade. 6. End your turn. PROPERTIES & HOUSES Property List: - Energy District - Cyber Market - Drone Hub - Orbital Elevator - Super Hub - AI District - Fusion Zone - Bank - North Station - South Station Buying a Property: - If unowned, you may buy it for the listed cost. - If owned by another player, you must pay rent. House & Hotel System: Level 1 — Cost £500K, Rent £250K, Upgrade £1M Level 2 — Cost £1M, Rent £500K, Upgrade £2M Level 3 — Cost £5M, Rent £2M, Upgrade £5M Level 4 — Cost £10M, Rent £5M, Upgrade £10M Level 5 — Cost £50M, Rent £25M, Upgrade £50M Roland’s House Example: - Roland pays £500K when landing on a Level 1 house. - Roland pays £5M when landing on a Level 3 house. - If Roland owns the house, he collects rent instead. TELEPORT & ACTION CARDS Quantum Jump — Move to any square instantly — £1M Energy Surge — Move forward 10 spaces — £500K Wormhole Slip — Swap places with any player — £2M Orbital Drop — Move to Orbital Ring — £5M AI Redirect — Move to Cyber Market — £1M Drone Lift — Move to your house — £500K Time Rift — Take two turns — £3M Hyperloop Override — Free Hyperloop travel — £1M Quantum Recall — Return to last square — £500K Teleport Lockdown — Freeze another player for one turn — £2M HYPERLOOP SYSTEM - Cost: £50M per ride. - Travel to any station instantly. - Hyperloop Override card makes travel free. - Hyperloop Discount Module reduces cost to £1,000 forever. ENERGY UNITS Used for: - Teleportation - Activating certain cards - Minor upgrades Earned by: - Landing on Energy District - Winning challenges - Certain Chance cards QUANTUM TOKENS Used for: - Time jumps - Orbital access - High-level teleportation Earned by: - Landing on Quantum Zones - Winning major challenges - Completing missions CHALLENGE ARENA (UPDATED) Landing on the Challenge Arena triggers a duel. Choose one: - Chess - UNO - Dice Battle - Trivia CHESS REWARD: - Winner earns £10M. - Loser pays £10M. UNO REWARD: - Winner earns £5M. - Loser pays £5M. DICE BATTLE REWARD: - Winner earns £2M. - Loser pays £2M. TRIVIA REWARD: - Winner earns £3M. - Loser pays £3M. MONEY SYSTEM Denominations: £1,000 £5,000 £10,000 £50,000 £100,000 £500,000 £1,000,000 £5,000,000 £10,000,000 £50,000,000 £100,000,000 £500,000,000 £1,000,000,000 CHANCE CARDS Chance cards may include: - Gain money - Lose money - Move forward/backward - Teleport - Hyperloop bonuses - Energy/Quantum rewards - Debt penalties LOSE CONDITION A player is eliminated if: - They cannot pay a required fee. - They fall below £40 and owe more than £1,000. - They cannot repay borrowed money. - They hit a penalty they cannot afford. Last remaining player wins. PLAYER TOKENS Choose one: - Drone - Pawn - Chip - Orb - Hyperloop Capsule SETUP 1. Each player starts with £1,000,000,000. 2. Shuffle Teleport, Chance, and Hyperloop decks. 3. Place tokens on GO. 4. Place houses and hotels near the bank. 5. Begin play.
